Hulk

About

The Hulk is the alter ego of Dr. Bruce Banner, a brilliant scientist who, after being exposed to gamma radiation in a laboratory accident, transforms into a massive, green-skinned humanoid monster whenever he experiences extreme emotions, particularly anger or stress. As the Hulk, Banner possesses incredible superhuman strength, durability, and regenerative abilities, making him one of the most powerful beings in the Marvel Universe. Despite his immense power, the Hulk is often portrayed as a tragic figure, struggling with the dual identities of Banner and the Hulk and the collateral damage his rampages can cause. Throughout his comic book history, the Hulk has been both a hero and a misunderstood outcast, often finding himself at odds with other superheroes and government agencies.
