Halkeye

Hawkeye's real name is Clint Barton. He is a highly skilled marksman and a master archer, known for his accuracy and precision with a bow and arrow. Initially introduced as a supervillain, Hawkeye eventually becomes a member of the Avengers, Earth's mightiest heroes, after being inspired by their noble actions. Throughout his comic book history, Hawkeye has been depicted as a skilled combatant and a resourceful strategist, often using his archery skills alongside various gadgets and equipment. He has also been portrayed as a mentor figure to younger heroes and a close friend to many members of the Avengers.
